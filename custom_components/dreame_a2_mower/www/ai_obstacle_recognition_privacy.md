# AI Obstacle Recognition Privacy Policy

> Source: the consent screen surfaced by the Dreame app's
> **Capture Photos of AI-Detected Obstacles → Privacy Policy for
> Capturing and Transmitting Photos**. This is the text the app
> requires you to **Accept Authorization** for before the AI Photo
> toggle can be turned on. Bundled with the integration so it's
> visible without leaving the dashboard.

Thank you for using our AI Obstacle Recognition function. Before using
our services, please carefully read the "Smart Robotic Mower Privacy
Policy" and this privacy policy.

Please note that during your use of the AI Obstacle Recognition
function, your smart robotic mower may capture information about the
location of the device and related personnel in the vicinity, such as
information about people in your courtyard and the distribution of
items. In this process, we may collect some of your sensitive personal
information, such as facial recognition data. For the processing of
your sensitive personal information and the access to relevant
permissions, we will remind you to pay attention in **bold** form. If
you agree to this privacy policy, it means you allow us to process
your sensitive personal information in the manner described below.

We will provide you with one or more of the following services under
the AI Obstacle Recognition Alarm function to enable the robotic mower
to avoid obstacles, allow you to view obstacle photos, and help us
improve the accuracy of AI obstacle recognition:

1. **Automatic Obstacle Avoidance** — Through this function, the
   robotic mower can automatically avoid people, animals, and objects
   using AI.
2. **Obstacle Photography** — After enabling this feature, the
   robotic mower will take photos of recognized objects and push them
   to you via the App. You can view and provide feedback on whether
   the recognition is accurate.

Please note that if you place your smart robotic mower in your or
other people's private space, or choose to make it run during your or
other people's private activities, the videos, pictures, or radar
sensor data generated after the above functions are activated may
still contain your or other people's personal information such as
privacy, portrait, or facial recognition information. When using the
robotic mower, please comply with local laws and regulations that may
apply to video surveillance, and pay attention to the shooting angle
to avoid capturing images of public areas.

Please note that, in accordance with regulatory and compliance
requirements for transparency and information, when using the robotic
mower:

1. The robotic mower will provide a voice prompt every 15 minutes
   stating, "Video security is in progress".
2. The corresponding indicator light on the robotic mower will remain
   flashing.

The AI Obstacle Recognition function will start running only after you
choose to turn it on. You can choose to turn it off at any time, and
the AI Obstacle Recognition function will stop running after it is
turned off. The image information generated by your use of this
function will be sent to your App account and presented in the form of
pictures. To implement the AI Obstacle Recognition function, we need
you to turn on the camera permission of the smart robotic mower in
your App. When you use the "AI Obstacle Recognition", your smart
robotic mower will detect and classify the human, animals, and objects
through algorithms, identify human characteristics, and may take
pictures. The legal basis for processing your personal information is
your consent. You can withdraw your consent in accordance with the
"Smart robotic Mower Privacy Policy". If you refuse to turn on the
relevant permissions or refuse us to collect this information, we will
not be able to provide you with the relevant services of the AI
Obstacle Recognition, but it will not affect your normal use of the
basic functions of the smart robotic mower.

To ensure the security of your personal information to the greatest
extent, if you use our AI Obstacle Recognition function, the obstacle
pictures recognized by the AI of your smart robotic mower will be
displayed to you on the App through our server located in Germany in
the form of a media stream in an encrypted environment. During this
process, we will not collect or store any of your video information.
If you use our AI Obstacle Recognition Alarm function, the videos and
pictures you actively capture will be encrypted and stored on our
cloud servers, Alibaba Cloud, located in Germany. We will not have
access to your video or picture information. Before the original human
shape pictures of you or others are uploaded to our cloud servers, we
will anonymize the aforementioned pictures, ensuring that even when
combined with other information, the anonymized pictures cannot
identify individuals. You can view, download, or delete the videos and
pictures you captured in the "Album" at any time. You can delete it
yourself through the APP or contact us to help you delete it at any
time. If you do not proactively delete them, the aforementioned videos
and pictures will be stored on the cloud server for 30 days and will
be automatically deleted afterward. We promise not to use the obstacle
pictures recognized by AI under this function for other purposes, and
we will adopt necessary technical and organizational measures, such as
SHA256 encryption, rootfs verification, AES encryption, etc. to
protect the security of your or others' personal information. We will
only use the non-human-shaped pictures you have provided regarding
inaccurate recognition. By inputting the non-human-shaped pictures
into the algorithm, the system will conduct autonomous learning to
help improve the accuracy of AI Obstacle Recognition. And you have the
right at any time, either through the APP or by contacting us, to
refuse to participate in the aforementioned artificial intelligence
training and product improvement.

At the same time, we fully respect your rights to personal information.
For the personal information collected from your use of this function,
you can exercise the right to delete and other rights in accordance
with the "Smart Robotic Mower Privacy Policy". You can also choose to
delete pictures and videos in the App. When you choose to delete in
the App, the pictures and videos will also be deleted promptly on the
server side.

If you have any comments or questions about this privacy policy, or
if you have any questions about our collection, use, or disclosure of
your personal information for this function, please contact us through
the contact information disclosed in the "Smart Robotic Mower Privacy
Policy"!

Thank you for taking the time to understand the privacy policy of
this function!
